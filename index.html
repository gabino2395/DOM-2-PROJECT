Lazy loading with Intersection Obvserver

App to implement the use of the Intersection Observer API. This way you use a lazy loading strategy where the user can add images but They will only be downloaded if they are in the viewport of the screen.

This popular method helps improve the loading time of websites, especially those that contain large amounts of data, such as images or videos,and this way the app will have a better performance.
